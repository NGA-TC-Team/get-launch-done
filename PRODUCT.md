# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Mobile app makers, indie developers, and launch teams preparing App Store and Google Play preview screenshots under release pressure.

## Product Purpose

StoreShot helps users turn raw mobile screenshots into a consistent 10-page store preview set with platform mockups, layout templates, localized copy, and ZIP export.

## Brand Personality

Focused, practical, and restrained. The workspace should feel like a production tool, while exported screenshots may remain colorful and store-facing.

## Anti-references

Avoid looking copied from The App Launchpad, avoid marketing-page decoration inside the tool UI, and avoid per-card controls that make repeated screenshot work feel noisy.

## Design Principles

- Keep the editor monochrome so screenshot colors remain the primary visual signal.
- Prefer direct controls for repetitive release work.
- Let each page use a distinct layout without forcing users through hidden interactions.
- Keep export settings centralized near ZIP export.
- Match device proportions to real hardware specs.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable Korean labels, keyboard-reachable native controls, visible focus states, and sufficient contrast for a dense production workflow.
